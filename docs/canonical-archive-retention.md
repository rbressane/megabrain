# Canonical Archive And Retention

Archive resources preserve reviewed evidence with stable provenance and immutable revisions. Use a resource body for approved Markdown evidence and a content-addressed manifest for non-sensitive binary artifacts. Preserve a pointer or snapshot, not a false copy of live transactional state.

Retention is explicit by authority domain. Supersede an outdated artifact with a new revision; retire material that should no longer appear. Git history remains, so retirement is not legal erasure. A privacy or legal deletion request requires backup inventory, repository-history remediation, object-store cleanup, and documented confirmation outside the normal tombstone flow.

Do not archive raw chats, sessions, prompts, personas, templates without durable knowledge, credentials, cookies, keys, recovery material, connection strings, or unreviewed security-sensitive documents. Sensitive evidence remains deferred until encrypted synchronization and backup retirement are independently reviewed.
